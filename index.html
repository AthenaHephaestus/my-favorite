My Favorite Hobby

Coding

I love to code. I've been doing it since kindergarten. At the time, my family and I were looking for good resources to code, and we came upon Scratch. From there, I've been going down the rabbit hole of coding, and here I am today, in WhiteHat Jr. Coding is the art of writing code and creating any type of online content, from art to animations to music. Coding is one of the most widely taught subjects in the 21st century, as its importance in the role of software engineering is large, and most parents and teachers want their children/pupils to be successful. Coding has many forms and iterations, from block-based coding like Scratch and Thunkable, to text-based coding like JavaScript and Python, to webpage-building code like HTML and CSS. Coding is not only fun, but it requires hard work and effort. That's why I love coding. You need to put in the effort before you reap the rewards.
